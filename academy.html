Who it's for

No unnecessary barriers.

Students, graduates, career changers, business owners, developers, designers, professionals, entrepreneurs — anyone willing to learn.

Learning philosophy

Videos alone don't build careers.

People need guidance, accountability, real projects, mentorship, feedback, and community. Everything in the Academy supports employability and practical application — not just watching lessons.

Delivery

Entirely online. Genuinely live.

  • Live, interactive sessions
  • Mentorship-driven, project-based learning
  • Small cohorts (15–25 people), so mentorship stays real
  • Community-focused, with recordings available
Pricing

Paid cohorts. A few seats we cover ourselves.

We price for people who are serious — payment is part of what keeps a cohort's momentum real. Alongside that:

Early-bird pricing — a genuine discount for enrolling ahead of each cohort, not a permanent markdown.

Merit-based seats — a small number of scholarship spots per cohort, awarded on ability, not ability to pay.

Corporate-sponsored seats — companies (including our own consulting clients) can sponsor employees directly.

Exact pricing is shared during application, since it varies by track. Free webinars always stay free.
