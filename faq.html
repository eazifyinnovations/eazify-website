FAQ

Common questions, answered plainly.

If something isn't covered here, just ask — we'd rather answer directly than leave you guessing.

What types of organisations do you work with?

+

Businesses, non-profits, faith organisations, and institutions of different sizes — from early-stage founders to established companies. What matters more than size is whether we're a good fit to solve the problem together.

How do projects begin?

+

Almost always with a Strategy Session — a conversation to understand your business before we talk about what to build. From there, we follow the Eazify Method™: Discover, Architect, Build, Integrate, Grow.

How long do projects take?

+

It depends entirely on scope — a focused website looks different from a custom platform or an AI integration. We'll give you a realistic timeline once we understand what you actually need.

Do you only work with businesses in Nigeria?

+

No. We're based in Abuja, Nigeria, but we've worked with organisations and audiences well beyond it. Distance isn't a barrier to a good working relationship.

Can you work remotely?

+

Yes — most of our work happens remotely, with regular calls and updates throughout the project.

Do you provide ongoing support?

+

Yes — this is actually core to how we work. We'd rather stay on as a long-term technology partner than disappear the day after launch.

How much do projects cost?

+

It depends on scope, complexity, and timeline. We don't quote blind — pricing is discussed openly once we understand the problem you're solving, usually during or shortly after a Strategy Session.

Do you build custom software?

+

Yes — internal systems, customer portals, dashboards, and mobile applications, all designed around how your business actually operates.

Do you help with AI adoption?

+

Yes — but we start by studying your workflows to find where AI genuinely helps, rather than adding it because it's trending.

Can we start with a Strategy Session?

+

That's exactly how we recommend starting. It's a low-pressure way to see whether we're the right partner before anything is agreed.

Still have a question?

Reach out directly — we'll give you a straight answer.
