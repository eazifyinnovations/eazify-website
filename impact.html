Faith & Mission

MCK Mission — Two Platforms, One Vision

Live

Challenge

Reaching people for daily prayer participation across language barriers.

Approach

Designed a focused, distraction-free platform built entirely around one daily action: showing up to pray.

Solution

A multilingual platform directing visitors into language-specific prayer communities, so nobody is left out by the language they pray in.

Outcome

A growing global community with a daily spiritual habit, in the language that speaks to them.

Challenge

Mission storytelling that builds a lasting connection, beyond a single daily action.

Approach

Designed a dedicated storytelling experience — separate from the prayer platform — built around narrative, not just utility.

Solution

A platform communicating the mission's broader work and journey, so supporters can follow the story, not just a single feature.

Outcome

An ongoing story people can follow, engage with, and support over time.

MCK Mission needed to reach people two different ways, so rather than force one platform to do two jobs, we built two connected experiences — each led by its own part of the vision.
