Faith & Mission

MCK Mission — Two Platforms, One Vision

Live
Pray for India Jesus for India

Challenge

MCK Mission needed to reach people in two different ways — daily prayer participation across language barriers, and mission storytelling that builds lasting connection.

Approach

Rather than force one platform to do two jobs, we designed two connected experiences, each built around a distinct part of the mission's vision.

Solution

Pray for India is a multilingual platform directing visitors into language-specific prayer communities. Jesus for India is a dedicated storytelling platform communicating the mission's broader work and journey.

Outcome

MCK Mission now reaches a global audience two ways — daily spiritual participation, and an ongoing story people can follow and support.
